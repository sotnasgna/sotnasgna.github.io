About Us

Welcome to Little Ghost - Where Creativity Meets Caffeine!

At Little Ghost, we've blended two of life's greatest pleasures – coffee and creativity – to create a unique and vibrant experience. Our pop-up cafe is not your typical coffee shop; it's a haven for artists, dreamers, and anyone who finds joy in the colors of life.
